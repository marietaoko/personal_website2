Henry and Grace

Every Friday nights, they went home late because of dance practice, so their parents gave them the safest way to get home.

They were not to take another path under any circumstances.

For weeks, the Henry and Grace respected this path; however, came one day when something was different.

This day, it was raining so much, it was so cold, and they were so tired that they wanted to get home as fast as possible.

What would you do?

Henry and Grace got home safe.
They were cold and wet, but a good, hot shower and a delicious cup of hot chocolate helped a lot.
That night, they went to sleep happy, it was still a great adventure!

Returning home through the faster path, Henry and Grace came across a lot of bars and clubs.

At one point, they had to walk into a small and dark alley where they found an open door which was apparently leading to a place with loud music and bright colourful lights.

This place looked very interesting, and they felt that they could find something magical inside.

What should you do?

Henry and Grace entered the strange place and met all sorts of people who were from nice, to awkward, to creepy.
When they realized that something wasn’t right in this place, it was already too late.
Sadly, Henry and Grace’s parents wouldn’t see their children that night.
