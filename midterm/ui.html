User Interface

For this project, I believe that the interaction is key, I want the user to understand the interface as easily as possible and to have fun with it. Consequently, I want the interaction to be as instinctive as possible.

On each page, there will be different buttons, and the user will have the choice to click on anyone of them for different results. I think that the buttons will be in text form, so I want to play with color, animation, or scale so that the person can see right away that they can click on them.

I would like to use a horizontal scroll for this website because I associate it more with the reading of a story, and I think that it will reflect the paths that the characters are taking. At the end of each story, there will be a small summary of the outcome depending on how the user will have rewritten it.

At every step of each story, the user will be able to go back to the “home page” with the title of the website or to the “menu page” where they can choose another story. Also, I would like to add an “information page” with explanations on the website, what it does, its goals, and the sources used for the content.

Finally, I want the website to look exciting and be fun to brows. Accordingly, I am planning to play with animations in the backgrounds, speed and fluidity of the scroll, and delayed appearances of texts and images.
